Selected Projects

Reinforcement Learning Chess Bot (Knightmare Protocol)

Python PyTorch

This project investigates reinforcement learning for chess with an emphasis on learning from expert demonstrations. I began by implementing NFQ and DQN on a reduced 5×5 board to study learning dynamics and stability, then scaled to full 8×8 chess using Double DQN and dueling architectures. A supervised baseline was trained on approximately 8 million master-level positions and deployed on Lichess, achieving ~1600 Blitz Elo. Current work focuses on transferring supervised representations into reinforcement learning to improve sample efficiency and playing strength.
